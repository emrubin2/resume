Adoption Infographic

About: This infographic illustrates the trends of international adoption in the United States. After the Hague Adoption Convention in 2008, the United States has seen a sharp decrease in international adoptions. The Hague Adoptoin Convention is an important international treaty that was adopted to safeguard children in the international adoption process. The main purpose of this convention is to protect children from abduction and from being sold into various forms of extortion or slavery. However, the increased regulations has made it more expensive and time-consuming to adopt internationally. Check out this website for additional information on the Hague Convention.
