Lancaster County Hispanic Population

About: Lancaster City is known to Pennsylvanians for its large hispanic population. In recent years some have nicknamed Lancaster "The Spanish Rose City". In recent years the hispanic population has increased across the county. Should this nickname included the entire county or is the population still concentrated in downtown Lancaster. According to data from American Fact Finder, there seems to be growth in the hispanic population around West Hempfield, East Petersburg, and East Lampeter. However, a significant part of the county does not have a high hispanic population. Check out this article for an update on hispanic population growth in Lancaster
