Philadelphia Commuter Map

About: The Southeastern Pennsylvania Transportation Authority or (SEPTA) is the primary transit provider for Philadelphians. As the average commute times continue to grow in this region, there is a growing concern among residents about where SEPTA plans to expand their rail network. The following map utilzes census tract data to identify the percent of working residents who rely on public transportation for commuting purposes. The goal of this document to help city leaders make descisions on where to build additional High Speed or Regional Rail stations.
